# Recommendations for Masjid Sunnah Helsinki Website

**Website URL:** https://ebayismail20-create.github.io/masjid-sunnah-helsinki/index.html  
**Date:** February 2026  
**Status:** Very good first version (only ~6 weeks after grand opening!)

## Overall Impression
The site already has excellent bones: clean design, authentic Ahl al-Sunnah tone, strong community focus, and transparent donation section. It feels welcoming and purposeful. With a few targeted improvements it can become one of the best masjid websites in Finland.

## Priority 1 – Fix Broken / Placeholder Content (Do this first – 1-2 days)

- **Prayer times stuck on “Loading…”** (home + dedicated page)  
  → Integrate free **Aladhan API** (or IslamicFinder). Auto-updates daily + shows countdown.

- Replace all placeholders with real information:
  - Imam / admin names + short bios (About page)
  - Real phone numbers (preferably WhatsApp Business)
  - Real email address (info@masjidsunnah.fi recommended)

- Update donation progress bar to current real amount (currently shows 45 k€ / 100 k€)

## Priority 2 – Visual & Design Polish

- Add a strong **hero image** on the homepage (prayer hall or Malmi location at sunset)
- Replace placeholder images with real high-quality photos:
  - Interior (men’s hall, sisters’ section, classrooms)
  - Grand opening photos
  - Community activities (Arabic class, kids, iftar, etc.)
- Add simple top navigation bar:
  `Home | Prayer Times | About | Programs | Donate | Contact`
- Adopt Islamic color scheme: deep green + gold accents + clean white/cream background
- Ensure perfect mobile experience (test on real phones)

## Priority 3 – Content & User Experience Improvements

- Add **Finnish language version** (at minimum homepage + contact). Even a “Suomeksi” button with Google Translate is better than nothing.
- Embed Google Maps on Contact page + “Get Directions” button
- Add Event calendar (Google Calendar embed or simple list)
- Create “Visitor Guide” section:
  - How to join
  - Dress code
  - Parking tips
  - Women’s facilities
- Add social media icons in header/footer linking to active Instagram & Facebook

## Priority 4 – Technical & Growth Recommendations

- Buy custom domain **masjidsunnah.fi** (or .com) and point it to GitHub Pages
- Basic SEO:
  - Page title: “Masjid Sunnah Helsinki | Mosque & Islamic Centre Malmi”
  - Meta description (150–160 characters)
  - Favicon (green mosque icon)
- Donation page is already excellent → add QR codes for MobilePay (33822) and bank transfer
- Later: functional contact form (Formspree or GetForm – free)
