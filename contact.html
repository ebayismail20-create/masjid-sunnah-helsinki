Masjid Services

Marriage (Nikah) Services

Islam highly encourages marriage, calling it half of the religion. Masjid Sunnah provides Islamic marriage solemnization (Nikah) for couples who wish to marry according to the Quran and Sunnah.

What are the requirements?
Presence of the groom, the bride, the bride's guardian (Wali), two adult Muslim male witnesses, and an agreed-upon dowry (Mahr).
Do you provide civil marriage certificates?
No, we only perform the Islamic Nikah. You must register your civil marriage with the Finnish authorities (Maistraatti) independently.

Funeral (Janazah) Services

"Every soul will taste death." (Quran 3:185)

Losing a loved one is a difficult trial. Our community is here to support families in ensuring their deceased relatives receive their full Islamic rights regarding burial.

  • Guidance on dealing with hospitals/authorities
  • Arranging Ghusl and Kafan according to Sunnah
  • Performing Janazah prayer at the Masjid

New Muslim Support

Thinking about embracing Islam? Or have you recently taken your Shahadah? Alhamdulillah, you have a family here at Masjid Sunnah.

The journey of a new Muslim can be challenging but incredibly rewarding. We provide resources, classes, and one-on-one mentorship to help you learn the religion step by step.

How do I take the Shahadah?
It is a simple testimony of faith: "I bear witness that there is no deity worthy of worship except Allah, and I bear witness that Muhammad is the Messenger of Allah." You can say this privately, but it is recommended to do it with the community so we can welcome you.

Getting Started

  • Take your Shahadah with the Imam.
  • Receive a New Muslim Welcome Package (Quran, beginner books, prayer mat).
  • Join the "Foundations of Faith" weekly class.
  • Get paired with a mentor (brother for brothers, sister for sisters).
