Visitor Guide

How to Join

Walk in for any prayer — no registration needed. All are welcome to attend Jumu'ah on Fridays at 13:15.

Dress Code

Please dress modestly. Sisters are asked to wear hijab inside. Shoes are removed at the entrance.

Parking

Free parking available around Ylä-Malmin tori. Bus routes 67, 69 & 68 stop directly in front.

Sisters' Section

A dedicated sisters' prayer room with wudu facilities is available on the upper floor with a separate entrance.
