Welcome to Masjid Sunnah

Established as a central hub for Muslims in Helsinki, Masjid Sunnah operates upon the authentic methodology of Ahl Al-Sunnah Wal-Jama`ah. We offer a welcoming space for spiritual growth, classical Islamic education, and active community service.
