Sunday Quran School

Sundays 10:00 AM - 1:00 PM
Ages 5-15

Comprehensive Islamic studies, Arabic alphabet reading (Qaida), and basic Quranic memorization tailored for children growing up in the West.

Aqeedah Essentials

Tuesdays after Maghrib
Shaykh Abdullah

An in-depth study of authentic Islamic belief (Aqeedah), equipping adults with certainty and clarity regarding the pillars of faith.

Saturdays 2:00 PM
